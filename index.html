About Me

My name is Anshita Saini and I'm a current high-school junior, set to complete the IB diploma by the end of this year. I am currently involved in technology and STEM as the Founder and President of Technovation Club and President of Rocketry Club; I am also an intern at the Ubiquitous Computing Lab, where I am working on research with sonic gesture monitoring. With my community, I explore the intersection of computer science and art as Co-President of She Codes Art, a nonprofit aiming to increase the participation of underrepresented minorities in STEM through a unique art-based approach to teach coding.
