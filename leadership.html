Leadership

Vice President of She Codes Art

Perhaps my most enjoyable experience with technology and community outreach is my efforts to advance diversity and equity in STEM as the Vice President of the international nonprofit organization She Codes Art by teaching coding with a unique art-based approach. With this organization, I volunteer weekly at a nearby Jubilee Reach center and have held over 50 workshops and conferences with over 50 attendees, helping over 1000 students gain exposure to computer science and art. We have partnered with over 14 organizations and companies, including Microsoft, Boys & Girls Club, and Technifusion. I've further represented She Codes Art at multiple events for changemakers, from the Bill and Melinda Gates Teen Action Fair to the Women In Technology Regatta.
