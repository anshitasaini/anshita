Leadership

Vice President of She Codes Art

As Vice President of international nonprofit organization She Codes Art, I advance diversity and equity in STEM by teaching computer science through a unique art-based approach. I volunteer weekly at a nearby Jubilee Reach center and have led over 60 workshops and conferences with over 50 attendees. Overall, I've helped over 1000 students gain exposure to computer science and art. We have partnered with over 14 organizations and companies, including Microsoft, Boys & Girls Club, and Technifusion. I've also represented She Codes Art at multiple events for changemakers, from the Bill and Melinda Gates Teen Action Fair to the Women In Technology Regatta.
